- [Accessibility] In [AllMailListView.tsx](\/private\/tmp\/webui-surface-audit\/apps\/webui\/src\/components\/dashboard\/AllMailListView.tsx#L136-L147), each mail row is a clickable `<div>` inside an `<li>` with no keyboard handler, focus target, or role. That makes the primary row action mouse-only, so keyboard and assistive-tech users cannot open messages from the list unless they tab to the separate button.
- [i18n regression] In [KnowledgeBaseStatsCard.tsx](\/private\/tmp\/webui-surface-audit\/apps\/webui\/src\/components\/dashboard\/knowledgebase\/KnowledgeBaseStatsCard.tsx#L15-L23) and [EisenhowerMatrixPanel.tsx](\/private\/tmp\/webui-surface-audit\/apps\/webui\/src\/components\/dashboard\/knowledgebase\/EisenhowerMatrixPanel.tsx#L25-L34), date formatting is hardcoded to `zh-CN` regardless of `locale`. English and Japanese users will still see Chinese date formatting, which is a visible localization regression.
